## Professional Focus

- Perioperative Physiology | Examining how perioperative physiological changes are recognized and acted upon at the bedside.
- Patient Safety | Emphasis on recognizing perioperative risk, acting early, and identifying reperfusion-related physiological changes in real time through clinical monitoring to prevent avoidable complications.
- Medical Education | Cultivating the next generation of medical practitioners through clinical skills, reflection, and professionalism.
